About Me

Hi, my name is Ahmet Selçuk Özdemir.

I'm 29 years old, residing in Istanbul-Eyüpsultan. I have been working professionally as a software developer for about 5+ years.

I specialize in Backend Development. Leveraging the flexibility of .NET Core and .NET MVC, I build robust infrastructures that users can trust. Alongside my expertise in MSSQL for data management, I actively utilize modern technologies such as RabbitMQ, Elasticsearch, and Redis to engineer high-performance and scalable systems.

In an industry where continuous learning is essential, I approach every new project with the same excitement as my first day. My goal is not just to write code, but to leave a mark on the digital world by creating fast, reliable, and modern software solutions.

Beyond the technical realm, I enjoy engaging with arts and culture; attending theater plays and concerts is a significant source of motivation for me.
